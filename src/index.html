Welcome to IAF 2021

We are excited to announce and invite you to this year's Inclusiville Arts Festival, featuring exhibits and performances by the talented residents of our fair village. Come take in the sights, sounds, textures, aromas, and flavors created by your friends and neighbors. Bring the whole family for what is sure to be a wonderful and memorable day. Check out all the details below and register to reserve your spot at IAF 2021.

FAQ

All events are general admission, meaning that you are able to choose where to sit when you arrive. This works on a first come, first served basis.

IAF is an alcohol free, family friendly event held during the day. We welcome attendees of all ages, however, children under the age of 16 must be accompanied by a guardian.

Inclusiville Arts Festival allows guide dogs. If you find that you need assitance after you arrive at the showgrounds, there will be event staff available to assist you. Please indicate to the ticketing staff as you enter the grounds.
